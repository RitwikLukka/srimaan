Sri Parasara Badri Narayana Bhattar Swamy


Srimaan Trust founded by the Acharya of Vedic lineage, Sri Parasara Badri Narayana Bhattar Swamy, is a mission established to promote the standard of living of the society.

With caste, creed, sex and age no bar it serves the needy especially the younger generation to live a peaceful content life by mutual understanding and helps.

The whole Universe is supported and permeated by the Supreme Lord. Everything in this universe is part of His divine body. Serving any being becomes His service. Realizing this fact is spirituality. Spirituality teaches us to be selfless and rekindles love in our hearts. This love pushes us to action in serving the society selflessly.
